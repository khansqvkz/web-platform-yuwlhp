Discover the World of Dragon Ball Manga

Dragon Ball is a Japanese manga series created by Akira Toriyama. It was first serialized in Weekly Sh≈çnen Jump magazine from 1984 to 1995, with the 519 individual chapters collected into 42 volumes by its publisher Shueisha.
The story follows the adventures of Son Goku from his childhood through adulthood as he trains in martial arts and explores the world in search of the seven orbs known as the Dragon Balls, which summon a wish-granting dragon when gathered.
With its captivating storyline, memorable characters, and iconic battles, Dragon Ball has become one of the most popular and influential manga series worldwide, inspiring numerous adaptations, including anime, films, video games, and merchandise.
As of 2022, Dragon Ball manga has sold over 300 million copies worldwide, making it one of the best-selling manga series of all time. Additionally, there have been more than 70 video games based on the Dragon Ball franchise, offering fans immersive gaming experiences across various platforms.
Akira Toriyama, the brilliant mind behind Dragon Ball, passed away on March 1, 2024, leaving behind a legacy that continues to inspire and captivate fans around the globe. His creativity and imagination have left an indelible mark on the world of manga and entertainment.
