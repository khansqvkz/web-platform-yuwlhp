What are in these sports?

Basketball

Basketball is a team sport where two teams of five players try to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop, following rules like dribbling, avoiding fouls, and obeying time limits.

Soccer

Soccer is a team sport where two teams of eleven players try to score goals by kicking a ball into the opponent's net, using only their feet (except the goalkeeper), while following rules like no hand use, offsides, and fouls.

Criket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game where two teams of 11 players each take turns to bat and bowl; the batting team aims to score runs by hitting the ball and running between the wickets, while the bowling team tries to dismiss batters and limit runs, following rules like not bowling no-balls or wides, and ensuring fair play throughout the match.
